### Waste Management Challenges
- **Early Dumping**: People dump waste early because they can't wait for bins to fill
- **Irregular Collection**: Inconsistent garbage collection schedules in urban and rural areas
- **No Incentives**: Lack of motivation for proper waste segregation
- **Rising Emissions**: Increasing landfill emissions harming the environment

### Farming Challenges
- **Expensive Manure**: Natural manure is costly, making it unaffordable for small farmers
- **Chemical Dependency**: Overuse of chemical fertilizers degrading soil health
- **Missed Opportunities**: Organic waste not being converted into valuable resources
